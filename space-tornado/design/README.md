## Overview

The **Timber Torpedo** is a wooden rideable vehicle featuring an electric drive wheel system with caster wheel stabilization. Its sleek, spacecraft-inspired elongated oval design combines artisan woodworking with modern electric propulsion.

## Drive System

### Central Drive Wheel Cutout
- **Cutout Diameter:** >12 inches (to accommodate drive wheel)
- **Location:** Center of board
- **Purpose:** Houses electric drive wheel assembly
- **Note:** Drive wheel specs TBD

### Caster Wheels (x4)
- **Height:** 3 inches
- **Quantity:** 4 units
- **Arrangement:** 2 front, 2 rear (symmetrical placement)
- **Purpose:** Stability and smooth gliding support

## Design Features

1. **Aerodynamic Shape** - Elongated oval minimizes drag and provides sleek aesthetics
2. **Centered Drive** - Electric wheel in the center provides balanced propulsion
3. **4-Point Stability** - Caster wheels at corners ensure stable platform
4. **Wood Construction** - Natural material, customizable finish, durable
